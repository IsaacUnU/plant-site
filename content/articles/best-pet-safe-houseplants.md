### Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Are all houseplants safe for pets?
A: No, many houseplants are toxic to pets, so it's essential to choose plants that are non-toxic and safe for your furry friends.
Q: How often should I water my pet-safe houseplants?
A: Watering needs vary depending on the plant, but most pet-safe houseplants require weekly watering or watering every 2-3 days.
Q: Can I propagate my pet-safe houseplants?
A: Yes, many pet-safe houseplants can be propagated through division, cuttings, or leaf propagation, making it easy to share plants with friends and family.
