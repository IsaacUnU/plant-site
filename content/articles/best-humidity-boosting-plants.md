## Frequently Asked Questions
Q: How do I care for my humidity-boosting houseplants?
A: To care for your humidity-boosting houseplants, make sure to water them regularly, provide them with the right amount of light, and fertilize them occasionally. You can also mist them with water to help increase humidity levels.
Q: Can I use a humidifier instead of humidity-boosting houseplants?
A: While humidifiers can be effective, they can also be expensive and require regular maintenance. Humidity-boosting houseplants are a natural and low-maintenance way to increase humidity levels in your home.
Q: Are all humidity-boosting houseplants pet-safe?
A: No, not all humidity-boosting houseplants are pet-safe. Some plants, such as the Alocasia Polly and the Flamingo Flower, are toxic to pets and should be kept out of reach. Always research the toxicity of a plant before bringing it into your home if you have pets.
