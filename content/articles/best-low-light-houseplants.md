## Frequently Asked Questions
Q: What is the best low-light houseplant for beginners?
A: The Golden Pothos is a great choice for beginners, as it's easy to care for and can thrive in low-light conditions.
Q: Can I grow houseplants in a room with no natural light?
A: Yes, you can grow houseplants in a room with no natural light, but you'll need to provide them with artificial lighting, such as grow lights.
Q: Are all low-light houseplants pet-friendly?
A: No, not all low-light houseplants are pet-friendly. Some plants, such as the Flamingo Flower and the Lemon Tree, are toxic to pets and should be kept out of reach.
